Welcome to the new home for my writing, projects, and the things I'm reading and
watching. I rebuilt this site from scratch with [Astro](https://astro.build) so
that publishing is as simple as writing Markdown.

## What you'll find here

- **Blog** — notes on engineering, tech, and whatever I'm learning.
- **Projects** — things I've built or am tinkering with.
- **Books & Watching** — a running log with short takes.

> Adding a new post is just dropping a Markdown file in `src/content/blog/`.
